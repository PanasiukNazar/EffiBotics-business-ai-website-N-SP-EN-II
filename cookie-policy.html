Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience while using our AI-powered business automation services. Our goal is to provide an optimal browsing experience while ensuring that your privacy is respected.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your device and remember your preferences, which improves the functionality and user experience of our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website and services. They enable basic features like secure login and page navigation.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect data on how visitors interact with our website, such as pages visited and links clicked. This helps us analyze and improve our AI-powered business automation services.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences, such as language and location, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to track your browsing behavior to provide relevant content, offers, or advertisements based on your interests in AI-driven business automation.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To enhance your browsing experience on our website and AI services.
  • To understand how you use our AI-powered business automation tools.
  • To remember your preferences and personalize the content you see.
  • To analyze and improve our website and services based on user data.

Managing Cookies

You have the option to control or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website and limit the functionality of our AI services.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Manage your cookie preferences and opt out of non-essential cookies.
  • Request information about the cookies we use and how they are applied.
  • Request the deletion of cookies from your device, though this may affect your use of our services.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party service providers to use cookies on our website for analytics, advertising, or other purposes. These third parties are subject to their own cookie policies and privacy practices.

Data Protection

We take the protection of your data seriously. The information collected through cookies is handled according to our Privacy Policy and is only used for the purposes outlined above. We employ stringent measures to ensure that your personal data remains secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies and protect your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies or this Cookie Policy, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your inquiries and ensuring your satisfaction with our AI-powered business automation services.
